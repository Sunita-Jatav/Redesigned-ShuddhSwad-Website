The Shuddh Swad Story
A journey of passion, purity, and the pursuit of an Indian dream.

OUR MISSION

To be India’s finest snacks brand, delivering high-quality, hygienic,

and affordable traditional snacks that you can trust and enjoy. We are

not just selling snacks; we are reviving a legacy of purity.




THE BEGINNING : The Spark of an Idea

The journey of Shuddh Swad began with a powerful experience.

A young founder’s craving for a local thekua revealed the lack of hygiene in traditional snacks.

This led to one question: Why can’t tradition and safety coexist?

That realization during Chhath Puja sparked a mission

— to make pure, traditional, and hygienic snacks available year-round.
